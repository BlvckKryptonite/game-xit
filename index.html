What Is Game-Xit?

Game-Xit is a movement dedicated to educating people about the dangers of excessive video game use. We aim to provide resources, stories, and real help for those who need it.

Why Game-Xit?

  • Recognize the early signs of addiction
  • Access support systems that can help
  • Connect with professionals and community resources
  • Take control of your time and well-being
