About Game-zit

At Game-zit, we’re on a mission to bring awareness to video game addiction— a growing concern in today’s digital world.

Common Signs of Gaming Addiction

  • Spending increasing amounts of time gaming
  • Neglecting responsibilities at school, work, or home
  • Withdrawal symptoms when not playing
  • Lying about how much time is spent gaming
  • Loss of interest in other hobbies

Why Does It Happen?

Gaming addiction can stem from a variety of psychological and social factors—such as escapism, social isolation, or the dopamine rush from achievements and competition.

Getting Help

Recovery starts with awareness. Game-zit provides resources, professional referrals, and a listening ear for anyone struggling with excessive gaming.

We encourage you to take that first step toward balance and mental wellness.

Recognizing the signs early is key. There are support groups, cognitive behavioral therapy, and lifestyle changes that can help bring back a healthy relationship with gaming. Visit our contact page to speak to someone today.
